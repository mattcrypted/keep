How it works
01

Remembered on 0G

Tell Keep something and it is written to 0G with a content hash. Reload, switch devices, even wipe the server, and it comes back from the chain unchanged.

02

Provable, not trust me

Every reply carries a receipt. Open it and Keep re-fetches the record from 0G and re-hashes it in front of you, so you see it was never altered.

03

Yours to own

Mint any memory as an NFT on 0G. It lands in your private Gallery and follows your wallet, so the things you say are assets you hold.

04

A Sealed Market

Seal a memory, encrypted on 0G, and list it. Buyers pay in OG to unlock it, settled wallet to wallet on-chain. Sell your knowledge, keep the proof.
